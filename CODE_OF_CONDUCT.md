# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Plugin Gentleman to be welcoming, constructive, and useful for contributors at different experience levels.

Everyone participating in this project is expected to communicate with respect, assume good intent, and focus critique on the work rather than the person.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, kind, and specific in feedback.
- Welcome newcomers and help them understand project context.
- Keep discussions focused on the issue or PR being reviewed.
- Explain tradeoffs when disagreeing.
- Respect maintainer decisions about scope, quality, and release timing.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Dismissive or hostile review comments.
- Publicly sharing private security reports or private maintainer communication.
- Spam, trolling, or repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or PRs, block users, or take other action needed to protect the project community.

If you see or experience unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub.

## Review Standard

We optimize for clear, maintainable contributions:

- Critique code, not people.
- Prefer actionable feedback over vague objections.
- Ask clarifying questions before assuming intent.
- Keep the project safe for contributors who are still learning.
